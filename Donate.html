Donate with love.

Always give without

While millions of kids in India cannot 
afford the books they wish to read, there are privileged people who have the same books lying on their shelves-books that they have already read.

Collection

We collect used/new books from individual,schools,corporates, etc
who are willing to support the cause.

Categorization

We recycle books that are non-usable format and categorize the pending books into Genre, Class &
subject wise.

Distribution

We distribute books via book stalls 
at govt. schools and colleges also. We also provide books to credible NGO's in education domain.
